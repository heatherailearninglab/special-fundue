Before and After Prompting

See how the same task changes when the prompt becomes clearer, more specific, and more intentional.

Why this matters

Visitors can learn faster when they see the difference side by side.

Before

Write something about AI for students.

Vague purpose, no audience detail, no tone guidance, no structure.

After

Write a 120-word introduction for first-year college students explaining what generative AI is, why it matters, and one caution to keep in mind. Use simple language and a warm, conversational tone. End with one question to spark curiosity.

Clear purpose, audience, length, tone, and output structure.
